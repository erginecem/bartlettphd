This website aims to create a democratic platform to explore the doctoral research at The Bartlett. It uses UCL's website as its database and automaticly updates the list every week. If you are a PhD student at The Bartlett, but cannot see your image here, please reach out your administrator. Once your image appears at UCL's website, it will be automaticly added here one week later.

The code is written by Ecem Ergin, the faculty rep for the PhD students at The Bartlett. If something does not work properly it is on her. Please reach out to her with your inquiries.
